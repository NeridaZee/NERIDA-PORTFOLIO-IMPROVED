Ann Chealzee Nerida

Chealzee or mostly known by her colleagues as "Zee" is a student of AB Multimedia Arts in Lyceum of the Philippines University Manila. Since she was a young child, she dreamt on working as a production assistant and a graphic designer. She mostly draws ideas and inspiration for her work from the different movies she enjoys watching and video games she plays during her spare time. Zee had a great time and experience in editing their documentary films in high school, and she has been hooked on the industry ever since.

She is also the Vice President of their college organization: LPU Multimedia Arts Society from 2021-present. With this experience, her leadership skills had developed.



Her forte in the art industry is graphic desiging, photo and video editing, digital arts, and watercolor painting
